Privacy Policy for Radio Player

Last updated: March 2026
Developer: Guoqing Chu

1. Introduction

Radio Player ("we", "our", "the app") is a free internet radio streaming application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and session analytics — used solely to improve app stability and user experience.
  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID): used by Google AdMob to serve ads to free-tier users.

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Favorites and playback history: stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or sync this data to any server.

2.3 Location Data

  • Approximate location (country-level, optional): detected once at first launch to auto-select local radio stations. This data is not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the radio streaming service
  • Display advertisements (free tier only; no ads for Pro subscribers)
  • Process and restore in-app subscription purchases via Google Play Billing
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All user preferences, favorites, and playback history are stored locally on your device only.
  • No user account is required to use this app.
  • You can clear your playback history at any time from within the app.
  • To delete all app data, uninstall the app or clear app data from your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Radio Player is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to your device Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • Clear local data: Clear playback history from within the app, or clear all app data from device Settings
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at the email below

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
