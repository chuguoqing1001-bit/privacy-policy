Privacy Policy for Radio Player

Last updated: March 2026
Developer: Guoqing Chu

1. Introduction

Radio Player ("we", "our", "the app") is a free internet radio streaming application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, OS version, app version, crash logs, and session analytics — used solely to improve app stability and user experience.
  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID): used by Google AdMob to serve ads.

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Favorites and playback history: stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or sync this data to any server.

2.3 Location Data

  • Approximate location (country-level, optional): used to suggest local radio stations. This data is not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the radio streaming service
  • Display advertisements (free version only)
  • Process and restore in-app purchases via Google Play
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

5. In-App Purchases

This app uses Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases and subscriptions.

All payments are securely handled by Google. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information such as credit card numbers.

For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All user data (favorites, playback history) is stored locally on your device.
  • No account registration is required.
  • You can delete data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings
  • Clear local data anytime
  • Contact us to request assistance
